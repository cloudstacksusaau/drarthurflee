Dr. Arthur F. Lee

Dr. Arthur F. Lee is a board-certified orthopedic surgeon practicing in Cincinnati, Ohio for more than 24 years. Dr. Lee specializes in advanced technologies that minimize recovery times, eliminate pain and restore mobility. He provides comprehensive orthopedic care to his patients, including physical therapy and rehabilitation to aid in the healing process. Dr. Lee’s philosophy is that surgery should be the last resort, not the first course of action. However, should surgery be required, Dr. Lee will provide the most comprehensive medical care from pre-surgery to post-operative rehabilitation. Some of the many orthopedic conditions he treats are sports-related injuries; fractures; painful injuries to the neck, shoulder and back; and arthritis. Dr. Lee performs the latest in joint replacement procedures, including Birmingham Hip™ resurfacing and partial knee replacements, as well as computer-assisted total hip replacements and total knee replacements. For the most advanced, comprehensive orthopedic care in Cincinnati, call Dr. Arthur Lee at (513) 232-2663 or request an appointment online.
